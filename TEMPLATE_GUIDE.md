## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

**Images not loading?**
- Check that URLs are correct and accessible
- Try using `https://` instead of `http://`
- Some image hosts may block embedding

**Badges not showing?**
- Verify your username/handle is correct
- Check for typos in URLs
- Ensure special characters in URLs are properly encoded

**Stats not updating?**
- GitHub stats can take a few minutes to update
- Try clearing your browser cache
- Stats update once per day
